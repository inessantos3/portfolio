About me

Games have been an important part of my life since I was a child, and so has been making them. Started with point-and-click adventures in PowerPoint, before eventually moving on to messing with game assets and finally learning to make my own.

Today, I'm a creative game programmer with experience in 3D visuals, and an interest in bringing all sorts of fictional worlds to life.

    BeWitch VR

    Engine: Unity
    Team Size: Solo Developer
    Development Period: July 2025 - September 2025
    Features:

  • Complex concentration-based mixing logic, allowing for free customization of the potion effects;
  • Interactive environment including ingredient bottles, books and letters;
  • Uses a locally-hosted large language model (LLM) to generate character decisions;
  • Character potion requests are generated based on their current state using the LLM;
  • Characters form opinions based on the potions they receive, using the LLM;
  • Characters have predefined personalities and relationships, which affect their decisions and allow them to share opinions among themselves.

    • BeWitch VR

    Engine: Unity 6
    Roles: Solo Developer

    A virtual reality game prototype where you receive potion requests from the townfolk and concoct the solutions to their troubles (or cause even greater problems).

    A large language model is used to generate the people's requests, as well as their reactions to each potion. Read More
